Welcome to Area 3001

Area 3001 is a hackerspace in Leuven, and was founded on the July 16, 2013.


Projects

Feel free to browse our GitHub repositories and contribute to Area 3001 projects.
  • enstropy - Providing USB access to the stm32f4-discovery's TRNG
  • A.R.E.S. - Augmented Reality Entertainment System
  • knixx - Opensource KNX compatible modules
  • DUKTio - Microservices, webscripts for IoT, open data, and much more.
  • Jellyfish
  • LIFX ESP8266 implementation
  • LPD6803 driver for the ESP8266
  • Dedicated Collect and Go barcode scanner
  • ...
